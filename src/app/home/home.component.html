Home page
This is a home page. To start using your wallet, please check out the left side of the screen.
To go to your wallet, select Your Wallet section. You can create an expense in the Your Wallet section. Wallet statistics will be shown only if you have at least one expense created.
User statistics will show you your page with your expenses
If you have several users in the wallet, you can find their expenses in the Wallet Statistics - just press the User icon and you will see their expenses.
Categories section can help you look how much you have spent in particular category.

If you want to add new members to your wallet, you have to be wallet's admin. You will see Admin section in the sidebar.
